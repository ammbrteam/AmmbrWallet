Buying AMMBR with cash

The Ammbr Foundations has appointed BPC Law in Hong Kong as the escrow attorney for receiving cash on behalf of the Ammbr Foundation.

The minimum cash transaction that can be accepted is the equivalent of $10,000 USD. In order to procure AMMBR through BPC Law, participants are required to fulfill the necessary requirements for KYC/AML compliance, as well as accredition status.
